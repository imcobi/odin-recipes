How to Cook Fish and Chips

This classic British dish features crispy, golden-brown battered fish served alongside thick

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cut into thick fries
  • 1 lb white fish fillets (such as cod or haddock)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup cold sparkling water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. Preheat your oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350째F (175째C).
  2. Rinse the cut potatoes in cold water to remove excess starch, then pat them dry with paper towels.
  3. Fry the potatoes in batches for about 3-4 minutes until they are soft but not browned. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, salt, and pepper. Gradually whisk in the cold sparkling water until you have a smooth batter.
  5. Pat the fish fillets dry and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Dip each fish fillet into the batter, allowing any excess to drip off.
  7. Carefully lower the battered fish into the hot oil and fry for about 5-7 minutes until golden brown and cooked through. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  8. Increase the oil temperature to 375째F (190째C) and fry the pre-cooked potatoes again for 2-3 minutes until crispy and golden. Drain on paper towels and season with salt.
  9. Serve the crispy fish and chips together with tartar sauce, lemon wedges, and malt vinegar if desired.
